Age 19 • XJTLU Year 2 Student • Introverted

Core Goals

Wants to earn participation marks and share brilliant ideas without the anxiety of speaking flawed English in front of 30 peers and the professor.

Frustrations

  • Needs time to translate thoughts from Chinese to English.
  • "Extroverts always dominate the group discussions."
  • Fear of being judged by the academic teacher.

"I usually just nod during Seminars. I have good points, but by the time I formulate my sentence in English, the topic has already moved on."

Secondary User

Dr. Sarah (The Facilitator)

Age 35 • Language/Academic Teacher • Extroverted

🎤

Core Goals

Wants to accurately assess student understanding, encourage equal participation, and foster a lively academic debate rather than a monologue.

Frustrations

  • Asking a question and getting "dead silence" in return.
  • Hard to track who is actually contributing in large groups.
  • Traditional forums (like Learning Mall) are too slow and rigid.

"It breaks my heart when I throw out a fascinating topic and no one speaks. I know they have thoughts, I just need a tool to unlock them."
