05 • Journey: The Knight's Journey

Current Journey (Before Round Table)

Pain-point map showing what happens in a normal seminar before support tools are introduced.

PHASE 1

Before Class

Student prepares ideas alone but worries about speaking accuracy and speed.

PHASE 2

Discussion Opens

Confident speakers start first; quieter students hesitate to interrupt the flow.

PHASE 3

Suppressed Contribution

Student drafts mentally, deletes internal wording, and misses the timing window.

PHASE 4

Teacher Blind Spots

Facilitator sees silence but cannot distinguish reflection from disengagement.

PHASE 5

After Class

Student regrets not sharing, and teacher lacks evidence for fair participation feedback.

Future Journey (With Round Table Support)

Mapping Yifei's transformational experience after private drafting and low-pressure AI support are introduced.
