About Our Project

Our project focuses on climbing-related user needs and aims to design a human-centered digital solution for beginner support, community connection, and better access to climbing information and services.

We want to improve the overall climbing experience by helping users understand climbing basics, find suitable partners or trainers, and engage more confidently in the climbing community.

Project Personas

Persona 1: Li Wen - Beginner Climber

  • Age: 20
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: University Student
  • Background: Saw rock climbing videos on TikTok and wants to try, but has zero upper body strength and no gear.
  • Personality: Introverted, hesitant to ask strangers for help, easily discouraged.
  • Goals: Wants to master basic V0-V1 bouldering routes and build core strength without looking awkward in front of pros.
  • Needs: A safe, non-judgmental way to find beginner guides, rent equipment, and perhaps find another beginner to go with.
  • Pain Points: Gyms feel intimidating ("bro-culture"); doesn't know how to read route grades; falls off easily and feels embarrassed.

Persona 2: Ryan Li - Social & Improving Climber

  • Age: 30
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: PhD Student / TA
  • Background: Has been climbing for a year. Currently stuck at V3 difficulty. Views climbing as his main social outlet outside the lab.
  • Personality: Outgoing, analytical, competitive but supportive.
  • Goals: Wants to break through his plateau (target V5), track his climbing progress systematically, and organize weekend climbing trips with friends.
  • Needs: Advanced route beta (tips), connection with coaches or more experienced climbers, and community event organization tools.
  • Pain Points: Hard to coordinate schedules with current climbing partners; finger injuries from improper training; lacks a structured way to track route completions.

Mandatory AI Disclosure (10-80-10 Rule)

  • Tool Used: ChatGPT (GPT-4)
  • Purpose: Used for brainstorming and drafting the detailed descriptions, goals, and pain points for the two climbing Personas.
  • Human Effort: We provided the specific context of our CPT208 climbing project (beginner vs. improving climber) as the prompt. After the AI generated the content, we manually reviewed, edited the details to match our target demographics (e.g., Suzhou university students), and formatted it into the portfolio.
