Academic Conversation

Inspired by the legend of King Arthur, Round Table is an AI-powered ecosystem designed to eliminate the "monologue" in seminars. We provide every student an equal seat to speak, think, and contribute.

Target Personas

Understanding the silence to build better conversations.

Primary User

Yifei (The "Silent" Student)

Age 19 • XJTLU Year 2 Student • Introverted

Core Goals

Wants to earn participation marks and share brilliant ideas without the anxiety of speaking flawed English in front of 30 peers and the professor.

Frustrations

  • Needs time to translate thoughts from Chinese to English.
  • Fear of being judged by the academic teacher.
Secondary User

Dr. Sarah (The Facilitator)

Age 35 • Language/Academic Teacher • Extroverted

Core Goals

Wants to accurately assess student understanding, encourage equal participation, and foster a lively academic debate rather than a monologue.

Frustrations

  • Asking a question and getting "dead silence" in return.
  • Traditional forums (like Learning Mall) are too slow and rigid.

Survey & Interview Reflection

Grounding our design in real XJTLU seminar data.

Statistical Evidence

Language Anxiety 84%
Silent Periods > 1 min 67%

Key Interview Takeaway

"I'm afraid my grammar will distract the teacher from my actual point."

Reflection: The silence is often a "buffer" for cognitive processing and face-saving. Our MAS (Multi-Agent System) should act as a bridge, not just a recorder.

User Journey Map

The "Quest" of a student during a typical seminar session.

STAGE 1: DISCOVERY

The Prompt

Teacher asks a hard question. Yifei feels interested but hesitant.

STAGE 2: THE VOID

Silent Struggle

Silence grows awkward. Yifei tries translating her thoughts in her head.

STAGE 3: INTERVENTION

AI Catalyst

Round Table's Icebreaker Agent nudges her with translated keywords.

STAGE 4: VICTORY

Contribution

Yifei posts her thought. The class engages. Marks are gained.

Key Requirements & Prototype

Designing for meaningful engagement.

Functional Requirements

  • Real-time English-Chinese keyword scaffolding.
  • Automatic detection of discussion bottlenecks.
  • Persona-based nudge mechanisms for inclusivity.

Round Table Logic

Utilizing a Multi-Agent System where Icebreaker, Concept Monitor, and Devil's Advocate collaborate to facilitate discussion.
