User Research & Reflection

82%
Students anxious about English fluency
64%
Teachers report "Dead Silence"

"I have ideas, but by the time I've translated them into perfect English in my head, the discussion has already moved on." —— Year 2 Student Interviewee

Reflection: Our research shows the barrier isn't a lack of knowledge, but a high cognitive load caused by real-time translation and social pressure. Traditional forums fail because they lack the proactive facilitation needed for live interaction.

Issue #1: Language Anxiety

Students fear "losing face" due to grammar mistakes during live debate.

Issue #2: Monopoly of Voice

Extroverted or native-level speakers often dominate, sidelining other perspectives.

10 - 80 - 10 Rule

10% AI Ideation: Generating initial persona scenarios and pain-point brainstorming.

80% Human Design: We manually refined the MAS interaction logic, created the User Journey curves based on real XJTLU student interviews, and architected the UI layout to match XJTLU academic branding.

10% AI Refinement: Using LLMs to polish the academic wording of our key requirements.
